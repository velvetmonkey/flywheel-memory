## [[Lessons Learned]] and [[Process Improvements]]

**Presenter**: James Park (Artemis Program)

### [[What]]'s [[Working Well]]

**James Park**: "[[Three]] things that [[Are]] working exceptionally well with Acme:"

1. **Design collaboration early in the process**
   - Acme's engineers participated in preliminary design reviews
   - Manufacturability feedback prevented costly redesigns
   - FEA support accelerated validation cycles

2. **Transparent [[Communication]] on [[Schedule Risks]]**
   - Early warning on thrust structure material delay
   - Proactive [[Solutions]] (backup supplier [[Engagement]])
   - No surprises - we always know the real status

3. **Quality documentation packages**
   - Material [[Certifications]] complete and traceable
   - Inspection reports detailed and clear
   - Acceptance [[Test]] procedures aligned with our requirements

**Sarah Chen**: "This partnership is a model for how vendor [[Relationships]] [[Should]] work. Open communication, [[Technical]] excellence, and shared commitment to the mission."

**Tom Wilson**: "From our side, two areas we'd like to [[Improve]]:"

   - [[Required]] clarification calls ([[Added]] 1-2 days)

2. **Receiving inspection [[Feedback Loop]]**
